# Maintenance Strategy

The maintenance strategy is condition-based with conservative predictive elements. The system should recommend action only when evidence is understandable and repeatable.

## Action Levels

| Level | Meaning | Response |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Advisory | Early trend or isolated evidence | Monitor, inspect during next opportunity |
| Warning | Repeated evidence or meaningful deviation | Plan short stop or targeted inspection |
| Critical | High score or rising risk | Controlled stop if condition persists or safety is affected |

## Work-Order Guidance

Every recommendation includes:

- Diagnosis.
- Priority.
- Downtime class.
- Inspection steps.
- Suggested spares.
- Verification criteria after maintenance.

## Baseline Governance

Do not update the baseline immediately after a fault. Update it only after maintenance verification confirms the asset is healthy and operating conditions are representative.
